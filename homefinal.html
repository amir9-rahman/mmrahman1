    Welcome to RBX

    Here at RBX.HIRE our purpose is to connect the buyer to the seller. Looking to buy robux? An item? RBX.HIRE can point you toward a seller. We can even provide a scripter or builder to hire. See the Sign Up page to join.

    Components
    Scripter

    Creates a system that works behind your game. Different levels of scripts ranging from simple to complex can be provided.

    Builder

    A builder is the creator of everything you see in the game. From the weapons to the map the builder is responsible for designing these models.
    Robux

    Roblox's in-game currency that allowes the player to buy items, game access, and more. The currency runs the Roblox economy.
    Reseller

    A reseller aquires robux or virtual items and resells them to buyers. A reseller can use our website to sell their inventory.
    Economy

    Our website is a way for members of the Roblox community to come together and interact with the market. A user can buy robux, items, or hire a game creator.
